How to Play

Three simple steps to endless fun

01

Join the Party

Gather 2-8 players and use your phone or tablet as a game controller. Join the room and get ready to groove!

02

Fake the Lyrics

Listen to a song sample, then write a fake lyric that sounds real enough to fool everyone. The best bluffs win!

Real "We're no strangers to love"
Fake "We're no strangers to doves"
03

Vote & Win

Vote for your favourite lyric — get thanked if you vote a friend's fake, but remember: the truth always makes you a winner!
